Gangotri

  • One of the most revered temple towns of India
  • Home to the sacred Gangotri temple
  • A scenic destination located high up in the Himalayas
  • A trekker's paradise
  • Famous for Rudra cave, where PM Narendra Modi had meditated

What to Know

Best time to visit

From May to October is the best time to visit Gangotri. During winter months, the town remains closed due to heavy snowfall. The temple has been shut for the winter of 2021-22.
