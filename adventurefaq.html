Adventure Tourism FAQ




Q) Is there any Age Limit to participate in Adventure Sports?
To participate in any kind of Adventure Sports, you must be 15 years old or more. Some adventure sports have a minimum age limit of 18 years.

Q) Can a solo traveller do an adventure sport?
Yes, it is totally okay to be a solo traveller. You will find many like-minded people in the journey. In fact, more than 60% of tourists are solo travellers or groups of friends.

Q) What are the requirements to do Adventure Tourism?
There are no complex formalities for Adventure sports. You need to pay the fee for the sport you choose and follow the safety instructions told by the instructor.

Q) Should I have a Medical or Travel Insurance?
It is recommended to have medical/travel insurance with you before you pursue any kind of adventure sport.

Q) How do we get equipment for various Adventure activities?
There are various shops that sell and rent various equipment for adventure tourism. Even some e-commerce websites have also come into business with reliable services.

Q) Name a few famous Adventure Tourism in Uttarakhand.
The most popular Adventure sports in Uttarakhand are Skiing, Trekking, Rafting, Camping, Paragliding and Mountain Biking.

Q) What are the Safety measures available during Adventure sport sessions?
First, Instructors familiarise the user/ participants with the safety measures. However, in the case of a mishap, instructors are trained to administer first aid and basic medication.

Q) How much amount is required for any kind of Adventure sport?
There are multiple charges that are added in your Adventure Tourism session i.e. Camping Fee, Trekking Fee, Guides & Porter charges, etc.
